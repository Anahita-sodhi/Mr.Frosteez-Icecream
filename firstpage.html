About Us

Welcome to Mr. Frosteez - your ultimate destination for indulging in sweet, creamy delights! We take great pride in serving an irresistible range of delectable ice cream flavors, carefully crafted and made fresh daily to satisfy your cravings. Mr. Frosteez Ice Cream Factory was founded in 1998 by Manumeet Singh Sodhi, a passionate ice cream enthusiast with a vision to bring joy and happiness to people through his delightful creations. With an initial investment of 50,000 Rs and a humble 150 square foot area in Arjun Nagar, our journey began with a dream to create ice creams that would leave a lasting impression on our customers' taste buds. In 1999, our co-founder, Pradeep Mahajan, joined hands with Manumeet, bringing his expertise and a shared passion for ice cream making. Together, they embarked on a journey to elevate the ice cream experience to new heights and expand the company's potential.
