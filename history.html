History of Mr.Frosteez

Company Founded in 1998

Year 1998

The company was founded in the year 1998 by Manumeet Singh Sodhi with an initial investment of 50,000Rs. It started its operations in a humble 150 square foot area in Arjun Nagar.

Co-Founder Pradeep Mahajan Joins

Year 1999

In the following year, Pradeep Mahajan joined as a co-founder, bringing his expertise and vision to further expand the company's potential.

Company's Growth and Expansion

Year 2002

With hard work and dedication, the company started experiencing rapid growth and expanded its operations to serve a larger customer base.
